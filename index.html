Maymunarnika, Park Borisova Gradina, Sofia, Bulgaria

Some Info

In July, 2024 we will talk again about JavaScript with beer in Maymunarnika, Park Borisova Gradina. We'll start with welcome drinks, will pass by several lightning talks, by JavaScript gurus, and will conclude with networking.
Preliminary schedule:

  • 16:30-18:00 Check-in, Wellcome Drinks & Handshaking
  • 18:00 - 19:00 Opening
  • 19:00-22:30 Lightning talks (15 minutes each)
  • 22:30~ Beer and snacks, and friendly chatting
Some of the most well-known JS speakers will take part in this event. Hope to see you there!
